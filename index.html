Our History

Established by Bart Sr. in 1956 and now owned and operated by his son Bart Jr. Bart's Barber Shop is a classic upscale barbershop.

We offer a variety of services, catering to everyone. There is one thing that we have kept a tradition in our business is the Old Fashioned Hot Towel Lathered Face Shave. This is a lost art today, so come to Bart's Barber Shop to get what you can't get anywhere around. All at very reasonable prices.

We strive to bring the memories back, with solely the clientele in mind. People pay for quality - Barts is where you will get it. We are barbers who love our craft, providing top notch service that helps men and women Stay Sharp.

No appointments are neccessary. Walk-ins always Welcomed!
